Napoleon Grills

Napoleon takes the art of grilling to a higher level. Every feature, every material is engineered to give ultimate performance so that you can create unforgettable meals and memorable moments. And, like you, we’re not just some rookie behind the grill – Napoleon is a family-owned company with more than 20 passionate years of grill-making experience. Every great meal starts with the best ingredients. Start yours with the best grill out there: Napoleon.

Prestige PROTM Series Grills

Napoleon’s Prestige PRO Series proves that cars aren’t the only things that have style and performance in one package. This slick grill looks and performs like its name. The Prestige PRO series with Infrared Side and Rear Burners is a professional style grill, built with high-quality, high-performance features that make grilling easy and impressive.

Available in natural gas and propane.

Prestige® Series Gas Grills

The Napoleon Prestige series is the total grilling package: a cart model natural gas grill with the capabilities of an outdoor kitchen. With four main burners, and Infrared Side and Rear Burners, this grill allows you to use eight unique cooking styles. Not just durable on the outside, the same quality stainless steel is used under the lid for the basin, tube burners, dual-level sear plates and cooking grids.

Available in natural gas and propane.

Rogue® Series Grills

Napoleon’s Rogue Series is perfect for the seasoned outdoor cook who’s looking for a quality grill that provides all the features of a prodigious grill but takes up the space of something a little more portable. No other grill gives you such versatility at such an affordable price.

Available in natural gas and propane.

Patio Heater’s and Patioflame® Series

Gather with your family and friends around the warm, radiant heat from Napoleon’s outdoor heating series. With a Napoleon Patioflame Table or Patio Heater, you can have the campfire/fire pit experience in your backyard without the mess and work, or regulations, of a real wood fire. Bring the excitement of a custom outdoor fire pit to your home and experience the ambiance that only the glow of a fire can provide.

Grill Accessories

Upgrade your grilling accessories. From preminum covers to charcoal smoker accessories and maintenance, Napoleon has the grilling accessories to take your barbecuing to the next level.
